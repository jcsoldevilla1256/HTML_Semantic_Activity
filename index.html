Spots

1. Monasterio De Tarlac

Barangay Lubigan, San Jose, Tarlac.

The Monasterio de Tarlac is a Catholic monastery on top of Mount Resurrection, part of the Zambales Mountain Range on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. It houses a relic believed to be a fragment of the True Cross of Jesus.

2. Sitio Baag

San Jose, Tarlac

Discover the serenity of Sitio Baag in San Jose, Tarlac, where every step unveils breathtaking mountain vistas, creating an immersive experience for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.

3. Tarlac Recreational Park

San Jose, Tarlac

Tarlac Recreatioonal Park (also known as Jose V. Yap Sports and Recreational Complex), is located in San Jose, Tarlac. It is a recreational complex that offers a wide range of sports and outdoor activities.

4. Cafe Josefina

Capas, Tarlac

Savor the tranquil vibes and delightful beverages at one of Capas, Tarlac's charming cafes, offering a perfect escape for relaxation.

5. Sitio Dueg

Sitio Dueg, Brgy. Maasin, Tarlac

DUEG is a Pangasinense word for "Carabao". It is a mountain top view where you can find the real peace brought by the scenic nature view. This Sitio is actually a village of indigenous people. This place is worth it because of the very Fresh Air, Wonderful People and Amazing View.
